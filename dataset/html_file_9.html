NADRA
Operational Management
NADRA field operations are controlled through eight Regional Headquarters. Each Regional Headquarter has varying numbers of Zonal offices which are responsible for controlling NADRA Registration Centers operating in their area of responsibility. Number of Zonal offices and NADRA Registration Centers are dependent on population and size of the area. Each Region is controlled by Director General who is directly reporting to senior management of NADRA. Main responsibility of Regional DGs is to ensure that all applicants are handled with utmost customer care without compromising NADRA’s Registration rules/regulations/SOPs.
